Nocktik

Nocktik is a solo electronic house music production project from Swaziland. Captivated by Jazz and Soul music from an early age, he combined it with his love for electronic music to create the perfect foundation for his signature sound today creating a unique brand of conscious music and soulful psalms that uplifts and inspires.
