Mission


Vision

Only Make Waves envisions a world where art, passion, and music intertwine seamlessly to create a captivating and immersive experience for its audience. A world where minimalism reigns supreme, allowing for a purity and honesty in both sound and visuals. Through its diverse roster of talented artists, Only Make Waves Music seeks to push the boundaries of what is possible in the music industry, while maintaining a deep respect for the creative process and the power of art to inspire and connect people from all walks of life. The platform's ultimate goal is to make waves in the industry and leave a lasting impact on the world through the beauty of its art and the passion of its artists

Overview

Only Make Waves is a creative platform that celebrates art, passion, and music. Founded by Nakiso Dlamini and Wandile Mawelela with the aim of promoting minimalism and showcasing the work of talented artists and musicians. The platform produces music, artworks, music videos, and merchandise that embody its core values. Only Make Waves also has an experimental sound design project that focuses on music scoring and ambient sounds. The platform is committed to building a centre for scoring and ambient music production for the cinematography industry. With a strong emphasis on artistic expression and creativity, Only Make Waves is a unique and innovative platform for artists and music lovers alike. The ultimate goal for Only Make Waves is to become a prominent force in the music industry, recognized for its unique approach to merging art, passion, and music. The platform aims to provide a stage for emerging artists who share its values, and to foster a community of creatives who inspire and support each other. Ultimately, Only Make Waves hopes to contribute to the evolution of the music industry by pushing boundaries and challenging norms, while staying true to its commitment to minimalism, authenticity, and creativity.

Objectives

1. To discover and support talented artists who create unique and innovative music.
2. To produce high-quality music releases that showcase the artistic vision and passion of our artists.
3. To create visually stunning artworks, music videos, and merchandise that complement and enhance the music.
4. To curate events and performances that provide a platform for our artists to showcase their talents and engage with their fans.
5. To expand our reach and audience through strategic partnerships and collaborations with labels, brands, and media outlets.
6. To build a community of like-minded individuals who share a passion for art, music, and creativity.
7. To promote diversity and inclusivity in the music industry and support underrepresented artists.
8. To establish a centre for music scoring and ambient music production that utilizes indigenous sounds and ambiences to create an authentic sound.
9. To grow and evolve as a platform while staying true to our core values of art, passion, and music.
10. To maintain transparency and ethical business practices in all aspects of the platform's operations.
11. To continually strive for innovation and excellence in the platform's artistic output, business practices, and community engagement.
12. To promote and showcase talented artists who are passionate about creating art and music that resonates with our values of minimalism, simplicity, and authenticity.
