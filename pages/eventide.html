The EP "Eventide" evokes the liminal beauty of the evening's approach, when light bends into shadow, and the world drifts into stillness. The artist Echoform captures this moment of transition, where calm and contemplation reign, using ambient soundscapes that carry listeners into the ebb and flow of their own emotions.

1. Waves
This track mirrors the rhythm of the ocean, where each wave represents the endless motion of time, pushing and pulling us toward reflection. The sound flows in soft undulations, wrapping the listener in its embrace. The waves, much like thoughts at day's end, arrive, linger, and dissolve, allowing space for tranquility to rise. It invites one to surrender to the natural cadence of life.

2. Oasis of Peace
A meditative pause amidst the EP, "Oasis of Peace" is an auditory sanctuary, a retreat into the heart of stillness. It reflects Echoform's ethos of creating music that is both transcendent and grounding. This track feels like an ethereal escape, where the quiet moments of twilight allow the spirit to find calmness in the chaos. Echoform masterfully intertwines silence with sound, offering a serene space to rejuvenate.


3. Bred by Water
Water is the source of life, and in "Bred by Water," the track explores the transformative power of fluidity and adaptability. The sounds are gentle, yet powerful, like water reshaping the landscape over time. It connects to the EP's overarching theme of flowing transitionsâmuch like the eventide that is shaped by the boundary of day and night. This song draws deep connections between nature and the self, as though we too are molded by the forces around us.
