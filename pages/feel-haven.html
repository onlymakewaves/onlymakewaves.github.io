Feel Haven

'Feel Haven' is a house track that aims to create a sense of emotional and sonic sanctuary, inviting listeners to experience a feeling of safety, comfort, and creative inspiration. The title itself suggests a space or environment where one can feel at peace and at home, surrounded by positive energy and uplifting music. The track has a minimal and organic sound, evoking a feeling of simplicity and purity that contributes to the overall sense of tranquillity and stability. Nakiso may have been inspired by the desire to create a sonic haven that encourages listeners to let go of their worries and immerse themselves in a world of emotive and artistic expression. Overall, 'Feel Haven' aims to provide a sonic experience that allows listeners to escape from the stresses of everyday life and find refuge in the music.
