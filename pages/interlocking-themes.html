Interlocking Themes (EP)

Only Make Waves Music · Nakiso - Interlocking Themes

"Interlocking Themes" is a chill and emotional trip through ambient and deep house vibes. Each track connects with the others, creating a smooth and cohesive experience. Perfect for winding down or getting lost in your thoughts, this EP brings out the feelings with every beat.

1. When You Are Not Around
This track sets a mellow mood with its soothing melodies. "When You Are Not Around" captures the feeling of missing someone, blending soft synths and gentle rhythms to create a nostalgic and comforting soundscape. It's like a warm hug for the times when you feel alone.

2. I Need You
"I Need You" dives deeper into the emotions with its rich basslines and intricate beats. This track is all about the intensity of desire and longing, building up a groove that pulls you in. The melodies and rhythms come together to express that powerful need for connection, making it a standout moment on the EP.


3. Interlocking Themes
The title track, "Interlocking Themes," ties everything together. It's a smooth blend of ambient sounds and deep house grooves that flow effortlessly. Each element in this track complements the others, creating a unified and immersive experience. It's perfect for getting lost in the music and feeling the seamless connection of all the pieces coming together.
