Nakiso

Nakiso is a music producer based in eSwatini,he is deeply passionate about minimal art and minimalism, and this is evident in both his music and his artworks. With a lot of love for movie scoring and ambient music, Nakiso's music is rich with soulful ambiance and a plethora of sound designs, all of which highlight his admiration for the world of scoring.
