About Us

We're an innovative and dynamic community dedicated to promoting and supporting unique and original artistry through music and other forms of creative expression. Founded by Nakiso Dlamini and Wandile Mawelela, Only Make Waves focuses on the perfect execution of art and everything in between. Our mission is to help affiliated artists and creators reach their full potential and make a meaningful impact in the world through their art.
